Pokemon Top Trumps

Rules

Game Rules

      Player and opponent starts with a deck cards each

      Opponent always goes first

      Opponent reveals their top card and chooese the best stat available on that card

      Game then reveals the player's top card and compares the stats

      If opponent wins, then they go again

      If player wins, then in the next turn, player reveals their top card and selects their best stat by clicking the name of the stat

      If its a tie then the game check wether any cards have weakness again the other card

      If players card is weak against opponents card then they lose, and vice versa

      If neither is weak then whoever chose a stat that round loses

      At the end of each turn the cards that was just played is removed and suffled and placed at the bottom of winners deck

      The game ends when one player has no more cards in their deck
